Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 4 Slices Muenster Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons of Mayonnaise
  • 2 Slices of Cooked Bacon
  • 1 Slice of Raw Tomato
  • Skillet
  • Spatula

Instructions

Heat skillet to 375 °F. While it is heating up, place cheese and bacon in-between slices of bread. Spread 1 Tablespoon of mayonnaise on the outside sides of each slice of bread. (Mayonnaise is used instead of butter as it is healthier and makes bread crispier) Place sandwich on skillet and set it sizzle. Use spatula to check the underside every 30 seconds. Once it has turned golden brown, flip sandwich to other side and repeat. After both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted into sandwich and bacon, remove sandwich from skillet and turn off the heat. Remove one slice of bread and wedge slice of tomato into sandwich. Voila! Grilled Cheese!
